Case Study

University Automation Platform

A premium internal solution combining a custom CMS with a .NET automation layer to synchronize academic data across the university system, Moodle, Microsoft Teams, and a centralized Angular control interface.

Project Scope

A company-grade implementation designed for reliability, visibility, and operational control.

CMS Delivery

Website content management

A custom CMS gave the organization full control over website content and publishing workflows.

Backend Automation

.NET integration layer

A .NET service extracts student, instructor, and course data from the university system.

Workflow Orchestration

n8n synchronization

n8n coordinates synchronization with Moodle and Microsoft Teams on a managed schedule.

Operations Console

Angular control center

An Angular frontend provides monitoring, control, and execution visibility for the sync process.

Business Outcome

The platform reduced manual data handling, improved consistency across systems, and established a clear operational process for day-to-day management.
